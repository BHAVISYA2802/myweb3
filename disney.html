History of Disney

The Walt Disney Company was founded in 1923 by Walt Disney and Roy O. Disney. It started as a small animation studio and has grown into one of the largest media conglomerates in the world.

Famous Disney Movies

Disney has created some of the most beloved films of all time, including classics like Snow White, The Lion King, and modern hits like Frozen and Moana.

Disney Theme Parks

Disney theme parks are located worldwide, including Disneyland in California, Walt Disney World in Florida, and many others in Paris, Tokyo, and Shanghai.

Iconic Disney Characters

Disney is known for its magical characters such as Mickey Mouse, Donald Duck, Cinderella, and more.
